Rest, relax, and rejuvinate.



Stay with us at Blue Window - boutique villas located in the tropical & coastal town of Junquillal, Costa Rica.

The Villas


The villas are a blend of contemporary design with a taste of local handmade wood finishes , furnishings, and locally produced artwork. There are six villas with three floor plans, including one bedrooms, one bedrooms with lofts, and a large two level villa with two bedrooms.

Blue Window Caffe


Our cafe features the highest quality Costa Rican coffee, a variety of organic teas, local baked goods, and various cuisine. The cafe includes a large outdoor shaded dining area surrounded by plants, flowers, trees and the sounds of local wildlife.

Stay in one of the world's Blue Zones.
